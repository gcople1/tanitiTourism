Things to Do on Taniti

From hiking to cultural experiences, Taniti offers endless adventure for every traveler.

Outdoor Adventures

Explore Taniti's natural beauty with hiking, snorkeling, and water sports.

🏔️
Hiking

Mount Taniti Summit

Challenging 4-hour hike to the island's highest peak with panoramic views. Guided tours available.

🐠
Snorkeling

Coral Reef Exploration

Crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life. Equipment rentals and guided snorkel tours.

🏄‍♂️
Water Sports

Yellow Leaf Bay

Kayaking, paddleboarding, and jet skiing rentals. Perfect for families and thrill-seekers.

🐦
Bird Watching

Island Wildlife Trails

Over 200 species of birds. Guided birdwatching tours with expert naturalists.

🏖️
Beach Activities

White Sand Beach

Relaxing beach days with volleyball, beachcombing, and sunset watching.

🚲
Cycling

Island Bike Trails

Scenic bike paths around the island. Bike rentals available at major hotels.

Cultural Experiences

Immerse yourself in Taniti's rich heritage and traditions.

🏛️
History

Taniti Museum

Learn about the island's history from ancient times to modern day. Artifacts and interactive exhibits.

🎨
Arts

Local Art Galleries

Contemporary and traditional Tanitian art. Visit galleries in Taniti City and Merriton Landing.

🎵
Music

Island Music Festival

Weekly live music at the Taniti Amphitheater. Traditional and contemporary island sounds.

🕺
Dance

Cultural Dance Shows

Traditional Tanitian dance performances. Learn basic steps in interactive workshops.

🏺
Crafts

Artisan Workshops

Learn traditional crafts like weaving, pottery, and jewelry making from local artisans.

🌺
Festivals

Island Festivals

Join local celebrations, markets, and cultural events throughout the year.

Guided Tours

Expert-led tours to discover Taniti's hidden gems and must-see attractions.

🌺
Full Day

Island Explorer Tour

Comprehensive tour covering all major attractions. Includes lunch and transportation.

🏔️
Adventure

Mountain & Beach Tour

Hike to Mount Taniti summit then relax on secluded beaches. Perfect for active travelers.

🐠
Marine

Snorkeling Adventure

Full-day snorkeling tour with professional guides. Visit multiple reef locations.

🏛️
Cultural

Heritage Tour

Explore Taniti's history and culture. Visit museums, traditional villages, and sacred sites.

🚲
Bike Tour

Coastal Bike Adventure

Guided bike tour along scenic coastal paths. Includes bike rental and refreshments.

🌅
Sunset

Sunset Sail Tour

Romantic evening sail around Yellow Leaf Bay with champagne and appetizers.

Rainy Day Activities

Indoor and covered activities for when the weather doesn't cooperate.

🏛️
Museum

Taniti Museum

Interactive exhibits about island history, culture, and natural history. Indoor galleries.

🎨
Arts

Art Galleries

Visit indoor art galleries featuring local and international artists. Many with cafes.

🎭
Theater

Taniti Amphitheater

Covered outdoor theater with indoor backup space for performances and concerts.

🛍️
Shopping

Island Markets

Covered markets and indoor shopping centers with local crafts and souvenirs.

🍽️
Dining

Indoor Restaurants

Enjoy Taniti's cuisine indoors at restaurants with covered patios and full indoor seating.

📚
Library

Taniti Public Library

Free indoor activities including reading rooms, internet access, and cultural programs.

🎉

Taniti Activities Map

Interactive map showing all attractions, trails, and tour starting points
